About Calvin Yoon

I am a data scientist with a passion for advancing healthcare. I have a Masters in Integrative Bioscience and Biotechology (graduated Summa Cum Laude / Highest Honors) from POSTECH in South Korea and a Bachelors in Biomedical Engineering with a concentration of Cell & Tissue Engineering from the University of Rochester. In academia, my research was focused on developing imaging methods for characterizing the tissue microenvironment of cancer and inflammation. In the medtech industry, I have led multi-million dollar projects involving cross-functional stakeholders that optimized manufacturing quality and efficiency of Class II and III products through automation and digitalization. Now, I am seeking work as a data scientist, analyst, or computational biologist. I look forward to connecting with you.
